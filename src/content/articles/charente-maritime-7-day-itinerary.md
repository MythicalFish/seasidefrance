This simple 7-day itinerary balances beach days, culture, and low-stress family travel around Charente-Maritime.

## Day 1-2: Settle in and Explore Locally

Walk around village centers, stock up on market produce, and enjoy the pool or garden at your rental.

## Day 3: Ile d'Oleron Day Trip

Spend the day on the island's beaches and stop in small fishing towns for lunch.

## Day 4: Historic Sites

Visit nearby forts and old villages to add history to your beach holiday.

## Day 5: Royan Coastline

Choose one or two beaches and keep the schedule light for children.

## Day 6: Slow Day

Plan a rest day with local food, pool time, and an evening barbecue.

## Day 7: Flexible Finish

Pick your favorite spot from earlier in the week and revisit before departure.
