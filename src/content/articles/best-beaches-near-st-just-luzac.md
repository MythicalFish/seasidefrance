Charente-Maritime offers a mix of sheltered beaches, long sandy stretches, and lively seaside towns within easy reach of St Just-Luzac.

## 1) Grande Cote

Ideal for long beach walks and sunset views. This coastline gives families room to spread out even in summer.

## 2) Ronce-les-Bains

A practical pick for families thanks to calm water in many areas and nearby cafes.

## 3) Ile d'Oleron Beaches

A great day-trip option with varied beach styles, from broad surf spots to quieter bays.

## Planning Tips

- Start beach days early in peak season.
- Pack shade and water for young children.
- Combine beach time with nearby markets or old port towns.
